About Me

I am a software engineer and data scientist with a passion for solving complex problems using technology. My journey in tech began with a fascination for how data can drive decision-making and innovation. Over the years, I've developed expertise in machine learning algorithms, data analysis, and web development.

My academic background includes a Master's degree in Computer Science with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence. I've worked on projects ranging from predictive analytics systems to interactive data visualization platforms that help businesses gain actionable insights from their data.

I believe in the power of technology to transform industries and improve lives. My goal is to contribute to this transformation by creating solutions that are not only technically sound but also user-friendly and accessible.
